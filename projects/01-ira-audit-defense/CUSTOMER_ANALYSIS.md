## Who is the buyer (role, company type, size)
Two candidate buyer classes (channel choice is itself a kill-test):
- **Direct buyers (credit holders):** tax director / VP tax / CFO at clean-energy **developers, EPCs, IPPs, tax-equity investors**, and — independently liable per IRS final regs — **§6418 credit transferees** (corporates who bought credits via Crux/Reunion-type markets). ## Who is the user (may differ from buyer)
Tax/compliance analysts and associates who assemble certified-payroll records, compute apprenticeship ratios, and draft audit responses — today by hand. The engine displaces *their* manual labor whether sold direct or via firm.

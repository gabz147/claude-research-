### ADR-001 — Repository is the source of truth (2026-05-31)
**Decision:** All durable state lives in markdown + JSON in this repo. Discord and any vector store are mirrors/observability only.
**Reasoning:** Claude sessions are ephemeral; conversation history is lost. Files survive resets, crashes, reboots.
**Alternatives:** Vector-DB-as-memory (claude-mem) — rejected as *authoritative* store (opaque, lossy, hard to audit). Discord-as-memory — rejected (not queryable/durable enough, rate-limited).
**Tradeoffs:** Files need disciplined updates each cycle. Accepted.
**Consequences:** Every cycle MUST update memory before exit. Bootstrap reads files, nothing else.

### ADR-002 — Headless full-auto via `claude -p --dangerously-skip-permissions` (2026-05-31)
**Decision:** Unattended cycles run Claude in print mode with permissions bypassed.
**Reasoning:** True 24/7 operation can't pause for human approval. User explicitly chose full-auto.
**Alternatives:** Scoped allowlist (stalls on unlisted actions); manual trigger (not autonomous).
**Tradeoffs:** Claude can run any tool. Mitigations: project-scoped CWD, lockfile, full logging, Discord alerts, git history for rollback.
**Consequences:** Supervisor must constrain CWD and log everything; human reviews via Discord + git.

### ADR-003 — systemd timer as scheduler (2026-05-31)
**Decision:** systemd service + timer drives the loop. Cron provided as fallback.
**Reasoning:** Survives reboot, restart-on-failure semantics, journald logging. `systemctl` present.
**Alternatives:** cron (no crash-restart); long-running daemon (more failure surface).
**Consequences:** Deployment installs a user/system unit; supervisor stays scheduler-agnostic.

### ADR-014 — Net-new discovery: OPP-004 (EU Pay Transparency defense-record) ARCHIVE at the vacuum screen; refine the TASK-0017 heuristic (2026-06-16)
**Decision:** Ran a net-new discovery cycle (TASK-0018) on the EU Pay Transparency Directive (2023/970) — selected via the TASK-0017 heuristic as a *just-landed* forcing-function (transposition deadline 7 Jun 2026) with a *defensive sub-wedge* candidate (Art. 18 burden-of-proof reversal → contemporaneous per-decision pay-justification defense record, the OPP-001b shape). **Killed it at the vacuum screen** — scored **63/100 → ARCHIVE**, did NOT deep-dive, did NOT promote.
**Reasoning:** The disprove pass succeeded decisively before deep research (exactly what the ADR-012 vacuum-first discipline is for). Three points: (1) **No vacuum — the wedge is already owned.** The "defensible documentation / litigation-risk mitigation" framing I hypothesized as open is the incumbents' *existing marketing*: Syndio PayEQ was "designed by labor economists, statisticians, and lawyers… reverse-engineered lawsuit elements" and explicitly lists the EU PTD; beqom/PayAnalytics sells "defensible documentation… to mitigate litigation risks"; Trusaic runs an EU-PTD center. → CompWk 6, Defens 6. (2) **Workflow + channel are taken.** The one genuinely narrower sliver — *counsel-privileged, point-of-decision* justification capture — is a bolt-on feature to the HRIS/comp-approval stack incumbents already integrate (Workday/SAP), AND maintaining privilege requires counsel to direct it from day one → law-firm channel conflict (the OPP-001 problem; US OFCCP privilege analog confirms). (3) **Forcing-function deferred + telegraphed.** Enforcement is fragmented (only Italy/Slovakia in force by 10 Jun 2026; Germany no draft; France/Netherlands/Spain → 1 Jan 2027; Sweden paused, seeking renegotiation), AND the directive was *adopted in 2023* — incumbents had ~3 years to pre-position, so the 2026 effective date left no open vacuum.
**Refinement to TASK-0017 (the load-bearing output of this cycle):** The "(a) just-landed forcing-function" axis was **under-specified and mis-fired**. *Effective-date-recent ≠ vacuum-open.* A long-telegraphed regulation (adopted years before its compliance date) gives incumbents a head start, so by the time it "lands" the vacuum is already closed. The axis should read: **bias toward forcing-functions that are recent AND whose obligation type is genuinely novel/surprising (incumbents could not have pre-built), not merely forcing-functions with a recent effective date.** Stronger, separate signal: **4 consecutive regulatory-compliance forcing-function plays have now capped (OPP-001 79, OPP-002 71, OPP-003 72, OPP-004 63).** "Pick the newest regulation" is itself becoming an anti-pattern for this org — compliance SaaS is a structurally crowded category with fast, well-funded incumbents (Syndio/beqom/Trusaic/OneTrust/GRC suites) that pivot within 1–2 years of any public mandate. The only ≥80 the org has found (OPP-001b, 83) won on a *contested post-event defense + record-reconstruction* sub-wedge where **no incumbent had productized the artifact** — and even it is operator-gated. **Forward bias:** next discovery should either (i) leave regulatory-compliance entirely (operational/financial/contractual pain with a clear high-ticket buyer and no dominant incumbent), or (ii) only enter a compliance regime when the disprove-the-vacuum check passes affirmatively *first* — i.e., name the incumbents and show the specific artifact none of them sell — rather than assuming a "defense sub-wedge" is open.
**Alternatives:** Deep-dive PTD anyway to produce a fuller record (rejected — the screen already disproved the vacuum; deep research would burn a cycle to confirm an ARCHIVE; the vacuum-first discipline exists precisely to stop here). Score it MONITOR to keep it warm like OPP-002/003 (rejected — CompWk/Defens are materially weaker than those two; even a generous reading stays <70; honest gate is ARCHIVE with a narrow revisit trigger). Screen 2–3 more sectors this cycle before recording anything (rejected — the higher-value deliverable is the refined heuristic + the "stop defaulting to compliance regs" signal, not a forced fifth candidate; a disposable worker does one bounded cycle).
**Consequences:** Ledger gains its first **Archived** row (OPP-004, 63) with a narrow revisit trigger. Still no second ≥80 project; project 01 remains operator-gated (TASK-0014). TASK-0018 → completed. TASK-0017 updated in-place with the refined axis + the "compliance-regs-as-anti-pattern" signal; next discovery cycle must apply it (look outside compliance, or pass an affirmative named-incumbent vacuum check before entering a compliance regime).

### ADR-015 — Net-new discovery LEFT compliance (OPP-005 D&D recovery) and STILL capped at the vacuum screen → the binding constraint is "visible money pool," not "compliance" (2026-06-16)
**Decision:** Ran a net-new discovery cycle (TASK-0019) that **deliberately left regulatory-compliance** per the ADR-014 forward bias — picked **ocean-freight Demurrage & Detention (D&D) overcharge dispute & recovery**: operational/financial money-leakage, high-ticket B2B buyer (importers/BCOs/3PLs), with the FMC May-2024 billing rule as a *tailwind not the product*, targeting the winning OPP-001b shape (contested post-event recovery + record reconstruction). **Killed it at the vacuum screen** — scored **64/100 → ARCHIVE**, did NOT deep-dive, did NOT promote. Second consecutive Archived row.
**Reasoning:** The vacuum check disproved it before deep research. The hypothesized artifact — AI invoice-vs-contract/tariff overcharge detection AND contingency recovery — is **already shipped/marketed on BOTH sides**: prevention/visibility (Windward shipped a dedicated "D&D Automation" AI product Feb-2025; BuyCo connected to 97% of carriers; Cargoo; FourKites; Portcast; a whole tracking-software category) and contested recovery (nVision Global, OceanAudit.com "Refund Recovery Specialist", Cass, STB, Auditec — a mature freight-audit-&-payment industry already working on contingency, explicitly recovering D&D). → CompWk 6, Defens 5, AI 5. The FMC rule *lowers the dispute barrier for everyone*, commoditizing the dispute rather than moating it.
**The load-bearing learning (unifies ADR-012/013/014 — the real anti-pattern):** ADR-014 hypothesized that "leave regulatory-compliance" was the fix for 4 consecutive caps. **This cycle falsifies that** — we left compliance and capped anyway, for the *same structural reason*. The actual binding constraint is **"large, search-visible, named money pool," not "compliance."** Any pain you can quantify with a headline stat ("$15.4B/5yr lost to D&D", "$14B PFAS settlements", "10–15% of logistics spend") **already has funded incumbents on both the prevention and the recovery side** — the stat is the very thing that attracted them. Compliance regs were just one *instance* of a visible pool; D&D is another. The only ≥80 the org has found (OPP-001b, 83) did **not** win on pool size — IRA credits are a big pool too — it won because the *specific artifact* (contested post-notice audit-defense + record reconstruction) had **no productized owner**, a sub-wedge invisible to the headline stat.
**New selection doctrine (supersedes the TASK-0017 framing as the primary filter):**
1. **Named-incumbent vacuum check is now FIRST and UNIVERSAL** (not just for compliance) — before any deep research, name the incumbents on BOTH the prevention AND the recovery/defense side. If anyone markets the artifact, it is MONITOR/ARCHIVE. (This check has now correctly killed OPP-004 and OPP-005 cheaply — it works; promote it to the front of the funnel.)
2. **De-prioritize large, search-visible money pools.** If a McKinsey/vendor/agency headline stat exists for the pain ("$Xbn lost to Y"), assume incumbents exist too. **Hunt instead for pain that is fragmented across many small budgets, embarrassing/unspoken, or not yet a named budget line** — where no headline stat has yet recruited competitors.
3. **Target shape unchanged** (OPP-001b): a *specific contested-defense / record-reconstruction artifact with no productized owner* — but verify "no owner" affirmatively across BOTH prevention and recovery.
**Alternatives:** Score D&D MONITOR to keep it warm (rejected — CompWk/Defens materially weaker than OPP-002/003; honest gate is <70 ARCHIVE). Pivot mid-cycle to a fresh deep-dive (rejected — would sprawl and under-evidence the score; the vacuum-first discipline exists to stop here; the higher-value deliverable is this doctrine refinement + a seeded next candidate). Deep-dive D&D anyway (rejected — vacuum already disproved it).
**Consequences:** Ledger gains a second Archived row (OPP-005, 64). 5 consecutive caps (79/71/72/63/64) vs the one 83. Still no second ≥80 project; project 01 remains operator-gated (TASK-0014). TASK-0019 → completed. TASK-0017 updated in-place to subordinate "newest regulation" to the universal "visible-money-pool" anti-pattern. **Next cycle is SEEDED**: apply ADR-015 to a *hidden/fragmented-pain* candidate — leading seed = **construction delay / change-order claim-defense documentation** (contractors/subs/owners; massive but no headline pool; record-reconstruction-heavy: schedules/RFIs/daily logs; served only by services-model claims consultants, no productized owner — verify the vacuum first).
